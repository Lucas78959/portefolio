What is the BTS SIO?

The BTS SIO (Computer Services for Organisations) is a state-recognised diploma at BAC+2 level. It is aimed at those who wish to train in two years as a network administrator or developer to enter the job market directly or continue their studies in the field of IT. There are two distinct options, the SLAM option and the SISR option

SISR: SYSTEM AND NETWORK INFRASTRUCTURE SOLUTIONS

The Infrastructure, Systems and Network Solutions option trains professionals in of networks and computer equipment (installation, maintenance, security). By graduating from a BTS SIO SISR, you will be able to manage and administer a company's network and ensure its security and maintenance

SLAM: SOFTWARE SOLUTIONS AND BUSINESS APPLICATIONS

The Software Solutions and Business Applications option trains software specialists (drafting of specifications, formulation of requirements and specifications, development, integration within the company)
